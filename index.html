What did a day consist of

My day consisted of waking up, walking to grab breakfast from the Pancakery and going out to the Pier to shop. After walking around Pier Park I would then visit the beach and watch the sunset.

Activity Plan

  • Snorkel or paddleboard in the calm Gulf waters
  • Visit St. Andrews State Park for trails and wildlife
  • Catch sunset at Russell-Fields Pier
  • Browse shops and arcades at Pier Park
  • Relax with a beach read under an umbrella
  • Visit the springs around the city
